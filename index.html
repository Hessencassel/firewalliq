FRANK SAYS

On Government

Washington D.C. is just Hollywood for ugly people who get to use your credit card. The difference is at least Hollywood admits it's fiction.

On Politicians

I pay taxes so the government can tell me how to spend my own money. That's not a social contract. That's a hostage situation with a W-2.

On the Media

My filter broke in 1987 and I have been absolutely fine ever since. Unlike cable news, which broke in 1996 and has been getting worse on purpose.

On Common Sense

They called it the American Dream because you had to be asleep to believe it. Frank is wide awake. Has been since 1994. Grunge did that to a generation.

On Freedom

Political correctness is just tyranny in a cardigan. Frank sees through the cardigan. Frank also owns several cardigans. He's cold, not stupid.

On Elections

If common sense were an Olympic sport, Washington D.C. would be 0 and 535. We keep sending the same people expecting different results. There's a clinical term for that. Frank knows what it is.

On Spending

The government spent $47 million studying why politicians are full of crap. Frank could have told them for a Bud Light and twenty minutes on his front porch. Nobody called Frank.

On the 2nd Amendment

The Founders didn't write the Second Amendment so you could feel safe. They wrote it so the government would. Read the Federalist Papers. Not the tweet about them. The actual papers.

On Gen X

We were the latchkey generation. We raised ourselves, fixed our own problems, and didn't expect a participation trophy. Now we're watching you people run the country. Frank is not impressed.
