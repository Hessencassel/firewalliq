Everything you need to run your first audit

FirewallIQ is designed to be self-explanatory, but here is a complete reference for supported platforms, compliance frameworks, and how to read your results.

Getting started

Three steps to your first audit

01
Create an account

Copy your firewall running configuration to your clipboard. FirewallIQ accepts CLI output directly — no export scripts, no special formatting required. Then create a free account at firewalliq.io and choose a plan.

02
Paste your config

In the audit panel, select your firewall vendor from the Appliance dropdown. If you are unsure, FirewallIQ will auto-detect the vendor from your config as you paste it. Select your compliance framework, paste your config into the text area, and click Run Audit.

03
Read your report

Your report streams in within 60 seconds. Each finding includes a severity rating, the exact config line that triggered it, the specific compliance control it maps to, and copy-paste CLI remediation commands. A compliance score from 0 to 100 summarizes overall posture.

Compliance frameworks

Four frameworks, one tool

Select the framework that matches your audit requirement. You can re-run the same config against multiple frameworks without re-pasting.

PCI DSS v4.0

For organizations handling cardholder data. FirewallIQ maps findings to specific requirement numbers including 1.2, 1.3, 1.4, 2.2, 8.3, and 10.2. Requirement citations are included in every finding.

CIS Benchmarks

Vendor-specific CIS hardening guidance covering management plane, authentication, logging, data plane, SNMP, and VPN. Findings cite the relevant CIS control area by name.

HIPAA Security Rule

For healthcare organizations and their business associates. Findings map to 45 CFR Part 164 safeguards including 164.312(a), 164.312(b), 164.312(d), and 164.312(e).

NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5

For federal agencies and organizations following NIST guidance. Findings map to controls including AC-2, AC-3, AC-17, AU-2, CM-6, CM-7, IA-2, SC-8, and SI-2.

Reading your report

Every report follows the same structure

Every audit report follows the same structure.

Executive Summary

Two to three sentences summarizing overall risk posture and the most critical issue found.

Compliance Score

A score from 0 to 100. Scoring starts at 100 and deducts points by severity: 15 to 20 points per Critical finding, 8 to 12 per High, 3 to 6 per Medium, 1 to 2 per Low.

Findings

Each finding includes: severity level (Critical, High, Medium, Low), the compliance control it maps to, the exact config line as evidence, a one-sentence risk explanation, and copy-paste CLI remediation commands.

Passed Controls

A list of things the configuration does correctly. Useful for demonstrating due diligence.

Summary Table

A count of findings by severity level.

Frequently asked questions

Common questions

What config format does FirewallIQ accept?
Plain text CLI output pasted directly from your device. For most platforms this means copying the output of show running-config or equivalent. FirewallIQ does not require any special export format.
Is my configuration stored anywhere?
No. Your config is processed in memory by a serverless function and discarded when the audit completes. Nothing is written to a database, file, or log. See our security page for a full explanation.
Can I run the same config against multiple frameworks?
Yes. Paste once, run multiple times with different frameworks selected. Each run counts as one audit against your monthly limit.
What does the compliance score mean?
It is a relative indicator of hardening posture, not a certification. A score of 100 means no findings were detected for the selected framework. Use it to track improvement over time, not as a pass/fail certification.
How do Team plan domains work?
When you purchase a Team plan, the domain of your email address is registered. Anyone who creates a FirewallIQ account using the same domain gets unlimited audit access automatically. No invites or seat assignments required.
Can I export my report?
Yes. Every audit includes a PDF export button that generates a branded report with all findings, remediation steps, and the compliance score.
What if I find a bug or my audit fails?
Email support@firewalliq.io with your vendor and framework selection. Do not include your actual configuration in the support email.
